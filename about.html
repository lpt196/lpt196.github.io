Hi! I'm Lynn. I enjoy conceptual, experimental,and interdiscplinary design.

Lynn is an multi-disciplinary designer, strategist, and problem-solver.

Her work specializes in innovative and playful use of graphic, digital, and analog elements and marrying disciplines to create tactful and progressive solutions.

Her work is bold, incorporating design systems, digital interfaces, and aspects from diverse disciplines. She has received numerous awards for works of various mediums.
